Design a **premium, modern, highly interactive website UI/UX in Figma** for a storage service brand named **Avati Safe Storage**.

The design must be **clean, minimal, high-end, and trust-focused**, similar to premium tech brands. Emphasis on **clarity, motion, and conversion optimization**.

---

# 🎨 BRAND GUIDELINES

* Colors:

  * Primary: Black (#000000)
  * Secondary: White (#FFFFFF)
  * Accent: Dark Blue (#0B1F3A)
  * Highlight: Gold (#D4AF37)
* Style:

  * Minimal + premium
  * Strong typography hierarchy
  * Generous whitespace
  * Subtle gradients and glassmorphism (only where needed)

---

# 🧠 DESIGN GOAL

Create a **high-conversion website** that:

* Builds trust instantly
* Explains services clearly
* Drives quote generation
* Provides seamless customer dashboard experience

---

# 🖥️ WEBSITE STRUCTURE

* Full-width premium hero with subtle motion (parallax or video)

  “Secure Storage. Simplified Living.”
* Subtext:
  “Store your belongings safely with 24/7 security, climate control, and doorstep pickup.”
* CTA buttons:

  * “Get Instant Quote”
  * “Book Pickup”

### Animations:

* Smooth scroll transitions
* Button hover effects (gold glow or elevation)
* Micro-interactions on icons

## 4. 🤖 AI QUOTATION SYSTEM (KEY FEATURE)

### Step 1:

* Household
* Business
* Vehicle

---

### Step 2 (Household Flow):

#### Input Fields:

* House size:

  * 1BHK / 2BHK / 3BHK
* Floor number
* Service lift availability (Yes/No)

---

### Step 3: Item Selection UI

#### Large Items (Dropdown / multi-select):

* Refrigerator
* Washing Machine
* Cot (Single/Queen/King)
* Wardrobe

#### Medium Items:

* TV
* Mattress (foldable/non-foldable with size)
* Microwave
* AC
* Coffee Table

#### Small Items:

* Number of boxes required

---

### Step 4:

* Location input:

  * Map integration (pin location UI)

### Step 5: Output Screen

* Estimated monthly storage cost (₹)

* Transportation charges (₹)
